# LockAndKey

[![Discord Bots](https://discordbots.org/api/widget/391977843229458443.svg)](https://discordbots.org/bot/391977843229458443)


<body style="background-color:#5c5c5c;">
<p style="text-align:center;">
Lock&Key provides easy to use commands to create, edit, and remove temporary private voice channels.
Each user is allowed one voice channel to keep things simple.
Private voice channels are deleted ~3 minutes after everyone leaves so you don't have to worry about them cluttering your server.</p>
<h1 style="text-align:center;">Creating voice channels</h1>
  <p style="text-align:center;">It's easy! Just simply type <code>~vc</code> and follow the instructions provided.</p>

  <summary><b>FAQ</b></summary>
  
  <h2 style="text-align:center;">Help I can't invite people!</h2>
  <p style="text-align:center;">Make sure you are mentioning the user you wish to invite. Ex: ~vc invite @Lock&Key</p>
  
  <h2 style="text-align:center;">Why do my channels get deleted?</h2>
  <p style="text-align:center;">The channels are temporary and get deleted if no one joins, or everyone leaves. 
  It is possible someone may be deleting your channel however.</p>
